    San Francisco Bay Area Travel Guide

    As a Bay Area native, I wanted to create a travel guide for my friends that I myself would use. My goal was to create something that was clean and minimal without losing its warmth.

    Created December 2016 with Adobe InDesign; redesigned October 2017.

    The old version can be read here.

    Inspiration

    I'm a big fan of POPEYE magazine and Monocle's travel guides. Both publications are keen to focus local hotspots compared to stereotypical tourist fare, and always provide plenty of bright photographs and illustrations.

    I tried to mimic the grid system of Monocle and emulate the visual aesthetic of POPEYE without being a blatant knockoff of either.
