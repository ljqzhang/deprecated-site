  • About

    • Hello, I’m Leslie Zhang.

    By Christmas, I will be free from the shackles of academia after wrapping up my coursework in journalism and design at Northwestern University. In January, I’ll be interning at Instagram with their communications team.

    I’m interested in the intersections of design, media, politics and popular culture. I’m especially fascinated by what journalists have dubbed the Retail Apocalypse, the commodification of design in American society and the impending burst of the technology bubble.

    I’m a freelance fashion writer for clients including Grailed, Heroine, Notre Shop and Third Looks. I’ve written about everything from the move toward experiential retail spaces to the ‘memeification’ of clout goggles. You can read more here.

    I spend my free time watching Terrace House with my boyfriend, loitering in coffee shops, reading the news and trying out pilates classes. Eventually, I’d like to learn ceramics, how to sew and how to build my own furniture.
